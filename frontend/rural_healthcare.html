Our Mission

In rural India, over 65% of the population lacks access to quality healthcare. AI MedAssist aims to bridge this gap by providing instant, reliable disease predictions through a simple, voice-enabled interface accessible on basic smartphones.

Our system is designed to work as a first-response health tool — guiding patients on urgency, precautions, and when to seek professional care. This can save lives in areas where the nearest hospital may be hours away.
